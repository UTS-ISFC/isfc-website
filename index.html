ABOUT US

ISFC is a faculty-based society affiliated with UTS Finance Discipline Group. Our main objective is to enrich the university experience and support proactive and career-focused undergraduate international students with their professional development in the Finance industry.

We facilitate regular visa navigation workshops and networking events with successful international alumni, industry professionals and Faculty staffs to provide members with guidance and support to understand their visa options after graduation, adapt to the Australian work culture, expand their professional networks as well as explore multiple potential career paths within Finance. We also encourage our members to enhance their technical skills and gain exposure to the industry by attending financial programming workshops and case competition opportunities throughout the semester.

With a view to creating a community for people who share an interest in Finance, we are NOT restricted to Finance students only. Grab this chance now to be a part of UTS ISFC family, sign up for our membership below!
